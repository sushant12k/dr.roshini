Meet The Doctor

8+ Years of

Dr. Roshni Mishra is a distinguished dermatologist with 8+ years of specialized expertise in medical, surgical, and cosmetic dermatology. She is certified as a hair transplant surgeon, celebrity injector, and permanent makeup artist.

With fellowship training in facial aesthetics from Bangkok and advanced qualifications from Pune, Dr. Roshni combines medical rigor with aesthetic artistry. She is recognized for her expertise in complex pigmentation disorders and innovative aesthetic treatments.
